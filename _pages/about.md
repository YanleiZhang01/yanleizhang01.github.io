I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at [Mila – Quebec AI Institute](https://mila.quebec/en/), working with [Guy Wolf](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FxiJ3AQAAAAJ) and [Yoshua Bengio](https://yoshuabengio.org). I also collaborate closely with [Smita Krishnaswamy](https://krishnaswamylab.org)'s lab at Yale University, and I am affiliated with the [AIDOS group](https://www.helmholtz-munich.de/en/aidos) under [Bastian Rieck](https://bastian.rieck.me) at Helmholtz Munich.

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Queen's University under the supervision of Professor Andrew Lewis.

**My research focuses on developing novel machine learning methodologies informed by tools from advanced mathematics, with a focus on applications in biomedical discovery.** My current interests include:

- Optimal transport, generative modeling, and deep learning to explore cellular development and trajectory analysis
- Diffusion geometry and manifold learning for high-dimensional and single-cell data
- Graph signal processing and PDEs for molecular structure prediction
- Neural representation learning and brain activity investigation
